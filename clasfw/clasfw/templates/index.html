Formalism used

$$ R_T = \frac{1}{2}(|H_1|^2 + |H_2|^2 + |H_3|^2 + |H_4|^2) $$

$$ R_L = |H_5|^2 + |H_6|^2 $$

$$ R_{TL} = \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}(H_5^*H_1 - H_5^*H_4 + H_6^*H_2 + H_6^*H_3) $$

$$ R_{TT} = - H_1^*H_4 + H_2^*H_3 $$

$$ R_{TL'} = \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}(-H_5^*H_1 + H_5^*H_4 - H_6^*H_2 - H_6^*H_3) $$


$$ \frac{d\sigma}{d\Omega} = \frac{p_m}{k_{\gamma}^{ef}} \left( R_{T}^{00} + \varepsilon R_{L}^{00} + \sqrt{2\varepsilon(1+\varepsilon)} R_{TL}^{00} \cos\varphi + \varepsilon R_{TT}^{00} \cos2\varphi + h\sqrt{2\varepsilon(1-\varepsilon)} R_{TL'}^{00} \sin\varphi \right) $$ $$ k_{\gamma}^{ef} = \frac{W^2 - M_B^2}{2W} $$ $$ p_m = \sqrt{E_m^2 - m_m^2} $$ $$ E_m = \frac{W^2 + m_m^2 - M_B^2}{2W} $$
